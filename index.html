My Favorite Restaurants

Nothing brings people together like good food. I would like to share to you my favorite restaurants from the place where I grew up, in the Philippines. Whenever you decide to travel in the Philippines, try visiting this restaurants that I will recommened to you.

Best Places to Dine:

  1. Kuya J
  2. Yakiniku
  3. Tokyo Tokyo

Filipino Restaurants

Kuya J

Kuya J Restaurant, formerly known as “Ang Kan-anan ni Kuya J”, started as a humble eatery along the streets of Cebu. After some time, the Kuya J’s dishes spread not only in Cebu but in different parts of the Philippines. It is well-known for bringing in delightful comfort Filipino dishes, like crispy pata, halo-halo espesyal, kare-kare, bulalo etc.

What I like in this restaurant:

  1. Crispy Pata
  2. Sizzling Sisig with Egg
  3. Lechon Belly

Korean Restaurants

Yakiniku

Yakiniku Restaurant is one of the famous Korean restaurants in the Olongapo City, Philippines. This popular restaurant offers an authentic Korea cuisines alongside the star attraction of all-you-can-eat samgyupsal. Yakiniku serves up a variety of traditional Korean dishes, including bulgogi, bibimbap and kimchi. It provides a truly satisfying culinary for lovers of Korean food.

What I like in this restaurant:

  1. Pork and beef Samgyupsal
  2. Kimchi
  3. Bulgogi

Japanese Restaurants

Tokyo Tokyo

TokyoTokyo is a well-known restaurant chain in the Philippines. It offers a range of traditional Japanese meals with an emphasis on affordability and accessibility. Popular dishes including ramen, sushi, tempura, and donburi (rice bowls) are available on their menu. This restaurant aims to satisfy local palates while offering genuine sense of Japan.

What I like in this restaurant:

  1. Beef Yakiniku Wagyu Rice
  2. Prawn Tempura
  3. Red tea
